FAQs

Common questions about printer support

Why is my printer showing offline?

This is usually caused by a lost connection between your printer and computer. Our experts can diagnose and fix this in under 10 minutes.

How do I connect my printer to WiFi?

WiFi setup steps vary by model. Our technicians will guide you through the exact steps for your specific printer model and operating system.

Where can I download printer drivers?

Fill in the support form with your printer model and we'll help you find, download, and install the correct driver for your OS version.

No. PrinterFixPro is an independent third-party support provider. We are not affiliated with HP, Canon, Epson, Brother, or any other manufacturer.

How much does support cost?

Our support plans are affordable and transparent. Pricing depends on the type of issue. Our experts will explain all options before any charges apply.

Do you support both Windows and Mac?

Yes! Our technicians are trained to support all major operating systems including Windows 10, Windows 11, and all recent versions of macOS.
